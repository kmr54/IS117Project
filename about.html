What's this site all about?

In so few words...

This site is exactly what you would expect: a music blogging website where I choose a song of the day and share it with you guys. Inspiration can come from anywhere: a song heard in a cafe, a Spotify recommendation, or even a YouTube comment. It's as much for me as it is for everyone who stops by looking for a new favorite song or a community of people who share their taste in music or love of music. And who doesn't love music?
