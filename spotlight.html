Artist Spotlight!

Artists that keep popping up around here

Dominic Fike

Album: What Could Possibly Go Wrong?

You'll find many of Dominic's song on this month's list. He's an up-and-coming artist making his way to the top, who has collaborated with artists such as Halsey and BROCKHAMPTON. Check him out!

Harry Styles

Album: Fine Line

You may know him as a former One Direction member, but he is much more than that. Listen to his new songs and you'll see why he's made this list.

Bülow

Album: Crystalline

Bülow is a young German-Canadian artist who is starting to gain more traction. Check her out and you'll see why!

Rina Sawayama

Album: Sawayama

Rina Sawayama is a Japanese-British singer whose music is as fun and exciting as her fashion sense! See her hits in the making!

Oliver Tree

Album: Ugly is Beautiful

Is he a strange one? Most definitely. But the stranger the better. In no time you too will be singing "Miracle Man" at the top of the lungs.

COIN

Album: How Will You Know if You Never Try

Whatever you do, do NOT let COIN slip under your radar. With a new album dropped this year, you'll have plenty to listen to from this modern pop-rock band.
