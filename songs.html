December 1, 2020

Song: Cash Machine by Oliver Tree

If you haven't checked out Oliver Tree's new album, Ugly is Beautiful, then you definitely should. Don't let the bowl cut scare you! "Cash Machine" is just one of the many great songs on the album, with a wacky music video to go along with it in true Oliver Tree fashion.

by Kerri Reed, 12/02/2020
